About Us and our vision

In LEXA we focus deeply on meticulously selected projects. LEXA is a Boutique Investment Company offering you personalised investment projects.

In our perspective finding, assessing, and picking the projects are the main tasks we enjoy doing. Meticulously selecting the investment means you know your investments and you have full control over it.

We work side by side with the client ensuring value creation in order to ensure long-lasting relations. Our Credo is based on transparency, hard work, in-depth industry knowledge, and passionate commitment towards meeting our shared client goals and ambitions. We aim to develop genuine partnerships with our clients and strive to be immersed at every phase.

Our team of experts finds the opportunities, adjust them to our long term goals and plans.

LEXA is headquartered in Slovenia (Ljubljana) since there is a lot we can do in Small Switzerland enjoying the stable and prosperous economy while having access to the Balkan Region market and lucrative investment opportunities.

A low entry-level for investors is key for building trust and we have offers for different budgets and intentions. You could be guaranteed your funds are getting used for the investments you want, like, and believe.

Investment Services and Projects

The focus of the company would be mainly on Real Estate investments. The investments include: purchasing lands, obtaining permits to start the construction phase, and development of commercial and residential real estate. In addition, we intend to invest in startups and innovative businesses besides traditional short term trades and investments in stocks and liquid assets.

60 percent of the funds would go to Real Estate projects as the mid to long term investments;

20 percent would be the fund dedicated to investment in startups covering short term;

And the remaining funds should be invested in liquid assets including stocks, trades, and short term activities to sustain the ongoing expenses of the company.
