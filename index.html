About Us and our vision

In LEXA we focus deeply on meticulously selected projects. LEXA is a Boutique Investment Company offering you personalised investment projects.

In our perspective finding, assessing, and picking the projects are the main tasks we enjoy doing. Meticulously selecting the investment means you know your investments and you have full control over it.

We work side by side with the client ensuring value creation in order to ensure long-lasting relations. Our Credo is based on transparency, hard work, in-depth industry knowledge, and passionate commitment towards meeting our shared client goals and ambitions. We aim to develop genuine partnerships with our clients and strive to be immersed at every phase.

Our team of experts finds the opportunities, adjust them to our long term goals and plans.

LEXA is headquartered in Slovenia (Ljubljana) since there is a lot we can do in Small Switzerland enjoying the stable and prosperous economy while having access to the Balkan Region market and lucrative investment opportunities.

A low entry-level for investors is key for building trust and we have offers for different budgets and intentions. You could be guaranteed your funds are getting used for the investments you want, like, and believe.

We try to find the opportunities, cut and fit them to our long term goals and policies and also offer investors the best, safest and cheapest way to get a residence permit so that in addition to participating in lucrative projects, they can get a European residence permit for themselves and their family members.

LEXA is more an Investment Company offering tailored investment projects. According to the laws of the Republic of Slovenia, non-European citizens can apply for residency in Slovenia by investment in business premises including purchasing shares of an active Slovenian company worth 50,000 Euros, and on top, they have to be employed by the company which means they can be employed in Lexa while obtaining their Work Permit or Blue Card. With this strategy, the applicant would first receive a one-year Resident permit which would be renewed for two two-year terms and then could be promoted to the Permanent Residency.

  • The yearly salary amount should be paid to Lexa in advance and the company would return the payment through your monthly salaries excluding tax and insurance applicable fees.
  • There is no need to register a company and hire a local accountant or employee, as the investment is made in an active Slovenian company.
  • Obtaining a residence permit is fast and the investor's residence card will be issued within a period of 3 to 6 months.

The investor enjoys the benefits of residing in Europe, such as visa-free travel in the Schengen area and a work permit in the Union, while enjoying his profits and employment records. The amount invested by the issuance of company share stocks and as a result, they can be traded and transfer to the third party. How to receive the profit according to the agreement will be in one of the following two options:

  • The investor benefits annually at an agreed interest rate,
  • The investor contributes to the profits and losses of the company's projects and activities.

Obviously, the amount of profit allocated in the first method will be lower due to lower risk.
